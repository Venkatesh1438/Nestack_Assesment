Your job is to extract structured transaction information from unstructured text carefully and accurately.

- date (string) - Date in YYYY-MM-DD format (exact or inferred)
- category (string) - One of: food | travel | utilities | software | other

---

CONFIDENCE SCORING (CRITICAL):

0.1–0.39: Cannot be reliably extracted from text
0.0–0.1: Field completely missing; return null

GOLDEN RULES:
- Higher confidence for exact, verbatim data
- Lower confidence for inferred or ambiguous data
